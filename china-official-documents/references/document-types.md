# Official Document Type Selection

Baseline statutory document types under `《党政机关公文处理工作条例》`: 决议、决定、命令（令）、公报、公告、通告、意见、通知、通报、报告、请示、批复、议案、函、纪要.

## Fast Selection

| Need | Usually use | Direction |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Deploy work, issue requirements, forward/approve-forward documents, appoint/remove staff | 通知 | Downward or broad internal |
| Ask a higher organ for instruction or approval | 请示 | Upward |
| Report work, reflect situation, reply to higher-organ inquiry | 报告 | Upward |
| Discuss work, ask/answer questions, request approval between non-subordinate organs | 函 | Parallel or non-subordinate |
| Reply to a lower organ's request | 批复 | Downward |
| Record agreed matters of a meeting | 纪要 | Internal/broad circulation |
| Commend, criticize, convey important situation | 通报 | Downward/broad internal |
| Give views and handling methods on important issues | 意见 | Can be upward, downward, or parallel |
| Announce matters to society or a defined public scope | 公告/通告 | Public |
| Decide major matters, rewards/punishments, changes | 决定 | Downward/broad |
| Publish administrative regulations/rules or orders | 命令（令） | Statutory authority only |

## Common Distinctions

- 请示 vs 报告: 请示 asks for approval or instruction and normally needs a reply. 报告 reports information and must not include approval requests.
- 请示 vs 函: use请示 for a direct superior. Use函 when the receiving organ has no superior-subordinate relationship or when asking a competent authority to approve a matter under its function.
- 通知 vs 通告: 通知 is for specific organs or internal recipients; 通告 is for matters that must be observed or known within a certain public scope.
- 公告 vs 通告: 公告 is for important or statutory matters announced domestically or internationally; 通告 is more operational and scope-limited.
- 批复 vs 复函: use批复 to answer lower-level请示; use复函 to answer non-subordinate organs.
- 纪要 vs 会议记录: 纪要 is an official document communicating meeting results and agreed matters; meeting records are internal raw records.

## Rules That Often Prevent Errors

- One请示 should normally cover one matter only.
- Do not send请示 directly to multiple higher organs unless rules allow it; do not copy it to lower-level organs.
- Do not ask for approval in a报告.
- Do not issue commands, approvals, punishments, or mandatory obligations without authority.
- Joint documents should involve organs with relevant authority; determine the lead issuing organ before formatting发文字号 and signatures.
- If a local office has its own document-handling rules, follow those for routing, secrecy, urgency, numbering, and templates.

## Type Notes

### 通知

Use for work deployment, communicating matters, forwarding documents, requiring implementation, personnel appointment/removal, or convening meetings.

Common title formulas:
- `（发文机关）关于（事项）的通知`
- `（发文机关）关于印发（文件名称）的通知`
- `（发文机关）关于召开（会议名称）的通知`

### 请示

Use for matters requiring higher-organ decision, instruction, approval, or policy clarification.

Common body structure:
- Reason and basis.
- Matter needing approval or instruction.
- Proposed plan, alternatives, and risk/impact when appropriate.
- Ending: `妥否，请批示。` or `以上请示，请予审批。`

### 报告

Use to report work, situation, problems, implementation, or reply to inquiries.

Common body structure:
- Background or basis.
- Main work/situation.
- Problems and causes.
- Next steps or recommendations.
- Ending: `特此报告。`

### 函

Use between non-subordinate organs to discuss work, ask questions, request approval by function, or reply.

Common endings:
- Requesting: `特此函达，请予函复。`, `妥否，请函复。`
- Replying: `特此函复。`

### 批复

Use to reply to lower-level请示.

Common structure:
- Cite the received request title and文号.
- State approval/disapproval and conditions.
- Give implementation requirements.
- Ending: `此复。`

### 纪要

Use to record meeting decisions and agreed matters.

Common structure:
- Meeting name, time, place, host, attendees.
- Main discussion and agreed matters.
- Responsibilities, deadlines, and follow-up.
